FAQ

Do you charge any commission?
No. Brickloop Advisors is builder-neutral and does not charge buyers any commission.
Which builders do you work with?
We curate options across reputed developers in Bengaluru. Since we’re neutral, we shortlist based on your brief, not builder tie-ups.
Do you help with home loans and paperwork?
Yes. We assist with loan options, documentation, and coordination through to closure.
What is “micro-commercial” and who is it for?
Compact commercial assets—retail bays, cloud-kitchen pods, co-work suites—suited for investors seeking yield with lower ticket sizes.
Do you support NRI and first-time buyers?
Absolutely. We offer end-to-end guidance, including remote shortlisting and secure documentation workflows.
How quickly will I receive options after I enquire?
Typically within 24–48 hours once we have your brief (area, budget, need). Urgent turnarounds are possible—just mention it.
